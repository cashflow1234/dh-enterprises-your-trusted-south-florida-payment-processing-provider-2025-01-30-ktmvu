Frequently Asked Questions

What types of payments do you process?

We support all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and online transactions through our secure payment gateway.

How quickly can I start processing payments?

Most merchants are approved and set up within 24-48 hours. Our streamlined application process and dedicated support team ensure a smooth onboarding experience.

What security measures do you have in place?

We maintain PCI Level 1 compliance, implement end-to-end encryption, tokenization, and real-time fraud monitoring to ensure maximum security for all transactions.

Are there any long-term contracts?

No. We believe in earning your business every day. Our services are month-to-month with no long-term commitments or early termination fees.

What support do you provide?

You'll have access to 24/7 technical support, a dedicated account manager, and our local South Florida team for in-person assistance when needed.
